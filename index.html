Our Services

At Dog Days we offer a range of services for dogs from Walking, Boarding & Pet sitting. Each of The Dog Days services are tailored to our customers individual needs.
If you have a specific request or even if your pet requires medication, our teams are able to help. We have over 10 years experience caring for pets of all types and sizes.
Just because we are called 'Dog Days' doesn't mean this is the sum-total of our services, Oh No! We can also care for your small animals as well.

Daily Walks

Daily dog walks from short riverside walks to long countryside walks. All are free from roads with heavy traffic and sheep.

Home Boarding

Board your dog in a family home while you are away and let it eat our shoes instead of yours. Pets are looked after by a capable and experienced pet sitter.

Small Animal's

Our small animal care service is here for your rabbits, guinea pigs, chickens, fish and small rodents.
